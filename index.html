Marco Espejo Nunez

4-time Spain champion and world champion in Bachatart Fusion. From an early age fascinated by the music latina, in 2008 he settled in Spain where he discovered the world of dance. He began as a self-taught modeled on dancers of the time, among them a special place went to Juan Pachanga Matos, who took for his inspiration and the key to the development of his own style.

Evelyn Castro Artiles

She began teaching Latin Dance in 2005, completing her classical dance and private lessons in other areas, their interest focused mainly on the stage. She won 1st place in Ron Brugal Canarias (2007), 1st place in Salsa Open Canaries (2010, 2014), was a part of National team in World Salsa Open (2010, 2012 and 2014). In 2014 she began to be recognized at the national and international scene Bachata Sensual

Dorota Jastrzębska

Her adventure with dance began at the age of 6 years. For 13 years, she danced disco, hip-hop and classical dance. Then jazz, which remains true today, leading dance classes. The first meeting with salsa and bachata captivated her heart to such an extent that, along with jazz swallowed it without the rest. Hard work has resulted in several times world championship in disco and hip-hop.

Krzysztof Juraszek

his adventure with dance began in 2010. The first dance steps put in the rhythm of salsa, but at the beginning of 2011 fell in love with Bachata. In 2012 he won the Polish Championship at the Bachata Stars Poland in 2012 and in the same year represented the country at the Bachata Stars Europe held in Norway. Music education is a solid foundation and huge aid within the meaning of music.
